Identity concepts

A mark for rung

One idea runs through the name, the tagline, and the engine itself: a target is reachable by many rungs at different cost, and you run the cheapest rung that works. Every direction below is a ladder with one rung lit — the winner. All are SVG: ~1 KB, crisp at any size, and theme-aware.

01 · Ladder

The literal ladder

Two rails, three rungs, the middle one lit in signal-teal. The most direct reading of the name — unmistakable, and it holds together down to 16 px. The safe, confident default.

02 · Ascent

Ascending rungs

Drop the rails; keep the rungs as a rightward climb — part ladder, part bar-chart, part signal-strength. Leans into the cost-ranked idea (each rung a tier) and feels more like data/measurement. The most distinctive, least literal.

03 · Tile

App-tile monogram

The ladder in negative space on a teal tile — the strongest favicon / social-avatar / GitHub org form, where a filled shape reads better than thin strokes. Pairs with concept 01 or 02 as the "container" variant of the same mark.

// on getting a logo for rung

  • No logo-generator skill or plugin is wired into this environment, and I can't render raster art — but SVG is the better format for a dev tool anyway (vector, ~1 KB, theme-able).
  • These are hand-authored SVG. The wordmark is set in a monospace stack to echo that rung is a CLI command; a shipped identity would draw or license a specific face.
  • Any of these drops into the slots already left in mkdocs.yml (theme.logo / theme.favicon), the README, and a favicon.svg.
  • Tell me a direction (or a color / vibe shift) and I'll refine the winner into finished logo.svg + favicon.svg files and wire them into the docs site.
